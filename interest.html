3. My K-Pop Group

This is MAMAMOO, the first K-pop group introduced to me by my cousin. Mamamoo is a Korean vocal girl group consisting of four members: (left to right) Moonbyul, Solar, Wheein, and Hwasa. They debuted on June 19, 2014, under RBW Entertainment. Today (19/6/2024) is their 10th Anniversary! I love all of their songs.

They are acclaimed for their energetic stages and robust live vocals, earning them the nickname "BeLisMamamoo" which means "Believe and Listen to Mamamoo" from the Korean general public. Besides group activities, each member pursue their work career as soloist and have their own solo songs and concerts. My bias is Moonbyul, the group's rapper who is also great at singing. I love her song Memories ft. Onewe.
